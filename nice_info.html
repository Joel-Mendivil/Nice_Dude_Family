    What is the Aurora?

    The Aurora is an electric car that includes other features to enchance your driving experience. Here at Nice Dude, we are working hard to help shape the future of transportation through futurisitc features that were once called science fiction.

    Features

    With every Aurora, there are many features that are included to make your drive more enjoyable. Some features include a mini-fridge to store your favorite meals, a hover mode to escape the traffic jam, and more to be revealed later.

    Technical Stats

    On full charge, the Aurora is the most power-efficient and most powerful car out there in the business. Speeds can go up to 250 mph and can go for one month without having to recharge.
